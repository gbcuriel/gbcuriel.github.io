What is Blacksmithing?

BULL SHIT GO

The basic process is heating the metal in a forge until it becomes malleable so it to be shaped with a hammer and anvil. When the desired shape is achieved the metal is cooled locking in its new shape. A smith can forge an endless array of items, including durable tools, custom knives, and beautiful decorative ironwork.

Some of the commonly used tools are:

  • Hammer
  • Anvil
  • Forge
  • Tongs
