Gluttonous Chupacabra Brewing

At Gluttonous Chupacabra Brewing, we believe every drink should be a legendary experience. Our mission is to stalk the wild frontiers of flavor, capturing rare hops, mythical fruits, and secret recipes to create our one-of-a-kind beverages. We are a small-batch brewery dedicated to the art of the unknown, transforming whispered rumors of taste into refreshingly real craft beers. Our goal is to let you devour flavors you thought were only myths.

From our signature "Elixir" IPA, brewed with a mysterious blend of wild mountain hops, to our seasonal "Fable" fruit ales that change with the stories we hear, our tap list is always an adventure. We are the perfect stop for curious connoisseurs, craft beer collectors, or anyone looking to surprise their taste buds with something truly legendary.
